ABOUT

Over the summer, while we were researching for my post-secondary education pathway, we came across many resources and summer camps, which we could have utilized but wasn’t aware of before. They would’ve been useful for university preparation. During our research, we realized how scattered information was and was daunted by the university application process. In order to fill the void between secondary and post-secondary education, we have decided to create a website (using HTML and CSS from grade 9) which gathers some possible information detailing some opportunities on summer camps, jobs, university application process by interviewing Vic Park graduates, standardized testing preparation, etc. We hope that it can become a useful tool for students, especially for incoming grade 9s, so they can have a clear picture of what they wish to accomplish.
